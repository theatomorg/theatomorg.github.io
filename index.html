About

The Atom Organization will offer an all-in-one cryptocurrency platform with the goal of simplifying the use of cryptocurrencies in order to attract new money into the market. Recognizing that a vital aspect of ensuring mass-adoption of cryptocurrencies and therefore exponentially increasing profits will be the ease-of-use factor that comes with the all-in-one aspect that we plan to offer.

We're an ambitious team of three currently situated in Sweden with an in-depth experience in blockchain technology. We're planning on creating Atom which will be a cryptocurrency ecosystem that will house the following services:

  • Wallet
  • Exchange
  • Market Capitalization
  • Portfolio Tracker
  • Reward and Loyalty Program Operator
  • Communications Platform
